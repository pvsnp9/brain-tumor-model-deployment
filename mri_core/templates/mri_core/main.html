Coronal Plane

(Frontal Plane)It is X-Z Plane, perpendicular to the ground, which (in humans) separates the anterior from posterior, the front from back, the ventral from the dorsal.

Sagital Plane

(Median Plane) It is Y-Z plane, perpendicular to the ground, which separates left from right. The mid-sagital plane is the specific sagital plane that is exactly in the middle of body.

Traversal Plane

(Horizontal Plane) It is a X-Y-Z plane, parallel to the ground, which(in humans) separates the superior from the inferior, put head from the feet.
